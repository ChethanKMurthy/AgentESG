# ESRS E1 — Climate Change

ESRS E1 is the climate change disclosure standard under the European
Sustainability Reporting Standards. It is mandatory under CSRD for all
in-scope undertakings.

## Key Disclosures

- Transition plan for climate change mitigation, aligned with the 1.5 degrees
  Celsius pathway of the Paris Agreement.
- Policies related to climate change mitigation and adaptation.
- Targets: absolute GHG emission reduction targets covering Scopes 1, 2, and 3,
  with interim milestones (e.g., 2030) and long-term goals (e.g., 2050 net zero).
- Actions and resources allocated to implement the transition plan.
- Energy consumption and mix, including renewable energy share.
- Gross Scopes 1, 2, and 3 emissions in tCO2e.
- Carbon pricing used in internal decision-making, if any.
- Anticipated financial effects of material physical and transition risks.

## Materiality

ESRS E1 disclosures are subject to a double materiality assessment: impact
materiality (effect of the company on climate) and financial materiality
(effect of climate on the company).
